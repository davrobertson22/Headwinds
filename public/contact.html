What to include in a bug report

Bugs get fixed fastest when we can reproduce them. If you can, include:

  • What you were doing when it happened (opening a route, advancing the week, etc.)
  • What you expected versus what actually happened
  • Your device and browser (e.g. iPhone/Safari, Windows/Chrome)
  • A screenshot, if the issue is visual

Feature ideas and feedback

Much of what's in Tailwinds today started as a player suggestion on Discord. If you have an idea — a mechanic, an aircraft, an airport that deserves to be on the map — post it in the suggestions channel. We can't build everything, but we read everything.

For questions about data and privacy, see our Privacy Policy, or ask on Discord and we'll point you to the right answer.
