Contact

Found a bug? Have a feature idea? Want to argue about the best starter hub? Send us a message below, or join the community on Discord.

Send us a message

Fill in the form and it comes straight to the team. If you'd like a reply, leave an email address we can reach you at. For bug reports, the more detail the better — see the tips further down.

Join the Discord community

The Tailwinds Discord is the heart of the community and often the fastest way to reach the team. Players share strategies, report bugs, suggest features, and help shape where the game goes next — and the developers read it daily.

What to include in a bug report

Bugs get fixed fastest when we can reproduce them. If you can, include:

  • What you were doing when it happened (opening a route, advancing the week, etc.)
  • What you expected versus what actually happened
  • Your device and browser (e.g. iPhone/Safari, Windows/Chrome)
  • A screenshot, if the issue is visual

Feature ideas and feedback

Much of what's in Tailwinds today started as a player suggestion. If you have an idea — a mechanic, an aircraft, an airport that deserves to be on the map — send it our way. We can't build everything, but we read everything.
