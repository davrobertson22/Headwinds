Privacy Policy

Last updated: 28 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Tailwinds ("we", "us", or "the game") handles information when you visit and play the game at this website. Tailwinds is a free, browser-based airline management game. We aim to collect as little personal information as possible, and this page describes what is stored, why, and the choices you have.

Information we store

Tailwinds does not require you to create an account or provide your name, email address, or any other personal contact information in order to play.

The game uses your browser's local storage to save your game progress (for example, your airline, fleet, routes, and finances) directly on your own device. This save data stays in your browser, is not transmitted to us, and lets you pick up where you left off. You can clear it at any time by clearing your browser's site data for this website.

Cookies and similar technologies

A cookie is a small file placed on your device. We and our partners use cookies and similar technologies (such as local storage and device identifiers) to operate the game, remember preferences, and — where advertising is shown — to serve and measure ads. Some cookies are essential to the game functioning; others are set by third-party services described below.

Third-party advertising

We may use third-party advertising companies, including Google, to serve ads when you visit the site. These companies may use cookies and similar technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites.

  • Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on this site. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to this site and/or other sites on the internet.
  • Third-party vendors and ad networks may also use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website.
  • You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings. You can also opt out of a third-party vendor's use of cookies for personalized advertising by visiting www.aboutads.info or, in Europe, www.youronlinechoices.eu.

For more information about how Google uses data when you use our site, see Google's "How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services" page.

Analytics

We may use privacy-respecting analytics to understand aggregate, anonymous usage — such as how many people play and which pages they visit — so we can improve the game. This data is not used to personally identify you.

Children's privacy

Tailwinds is a general-audience game and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps to remove it.

Your choices

  • You can clear your saved game and any stored data by clearing this site's data in your browser settings.
  • You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings; note that disabling some cookies may affect how the game works.
  • You can opt out of personalized advertising using the links in the advertising section above.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, you can reach us through the Tailwinds community on Discord: join the Tailwinds Discord.
