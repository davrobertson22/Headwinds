Contact

Found a bug? Have a feature idea? Need to flag a player who's breaking the rules? Send us a message below, or join the community on Discord.

Send us a message

Fill in the form and it comes straight to the team. If you'd like a reply, leave an email address we can reach you at. To report a specific player, the quickest route is the in-game Report button — it tells us who and where automatically — but you're welcome to use the form for anything else.

Join the Discord community

Headwinds and Tailwinds share a community on Discord, and it's often the fastest way to reach the team. Players find worlds to join, team up for alliances, share strategies, report bugs, and help shape where both games go next — and the developers read it daily.

Reporting a player

Headwinds only works when everyone plays straight. If someone is cheating, harassing, running multiple airlines in a world, or otherwise breaking the Fair Play Rules, use the in-game Report button — it sends us the details along with who and where, so we can look into it. You can also describe the situation through the form above.

What to include in a bug report

Bugs get fixed fastest when we can reproduce them. If you can, include:

  • What you were doing when it happened (opening a route, sending a message, checking the standings, etc.)
  • Which world you were in, and what you expected versus what actually happened
  • Your device and browser (e.g. iPhone/Safari, Windows/Chrome)
  • A screenshot, if the issue is visual

Feature ideas and feedback

A lot of Headwinds took shape from player suggestions. If you have an idea — a mechanic, an alliance feature, an aircraft, an airport that deserves to be on the map — send it our way. We can't build everything, but we read everything.

Privacy questions

For questions about your account data and privacy, see our Privacy Policy, or ask through the form above and we'll point you to the right answer.
